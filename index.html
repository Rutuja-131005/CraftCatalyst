About Artisan Connect

Artisan Connect is an AI-driven platform developed in partnership with the Ministry of Textiles to empower India's traditional craftspeople. We bridge the gap between age-old craftsmanship and the digital economy.

Our mission is to preserve cultural heritage while providing artisans with modern tools to reach global markets, tell their stories, and build sustainable livelihoods.
